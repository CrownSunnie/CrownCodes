About B.Emp

This Mobile App 'B.Emp' is an abbrevation of the phrase "Be-Employed", this app is to will educate youths and career-minds on how the manner of approach towards Career. This app will help to reduce the unemployment rate in Nigeria, through the process of enlightening youths on their career, job, occupation, starting new bussinesses and also Interview Guidelines

Below are objectives of B.Emp Mobile App;

  • To educate youths on their take on career.
  • To help youths to know more about
    • their course of study
    • Companies they can work
    • Salary they can obtain based of level of education
    • New businesses that they can start with
  • To also help O'level graduates with bussinesses they can start with
  • Help youths to gain good place in their places of interview
  • Advertise Youth-Mobile

About the App developer

The Developer of this app is Olusesan Felix Gbolahan, a student of Computer Science.He is also one of those youths who have benefited from UNESCO good deeds, youth empowerment by Youth-Mobile. He aim and objective toward this app is to reduce the unemployment rate in his country Nigeria, this shows a level of patriotism and concern of the "youths well-being" in his country.
