Agriculture

What is Agriculture

In a lame-man language, it is planting of crops, harvesting them and rearing of farm animals

Agriculture is the cultivation and breeding of animals, plants and fungi for food, fiber, biofuel, medicinal plants and other products used to sustain and enhance human life.

Agricultural science is a broad multidisciplinary field of biology that encompasses the parts of exact, natural, economic and social sciences that are used in the practice and understanding of agriculture.

Career Scope of Agricultural Science

In today’s age of technology, it’s not always about a farmer having a subsistent or commercial farming bussiness. Agriculture is a big industry, and most of the careers are related to science and/or business. The agriculture industry plays a major role in the economy of most countries in the world. Hence, if you have the passion, skills and knowledge, there are several opportunities.
